A beautiful and professional way to showcase your services or products on your website

== Description ==

* This plugin allows you to add, edit, search and display your services on any page, or in a widget
quickly and easily. 
* It comes with a couple of different styles to choose from.
* Re-order services with a simple drag & drop.
* output your services members anywhere with the shortcode [our-services]

== Installation ==
1. Download the plugin, then upload the zipped file to your site from the WordPress dashboard plugin uploader menu
2. OR - Upload the unpacked folder folder via FTP into /wp-content/plugins
